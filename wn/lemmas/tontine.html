tontine.n.02 tontine "an annuity scheme wherein participants share certain benefits and on the death of any participant his benefits are redistributed among the remaining participants; can run for a fixed period of time or until the death of all but one participant"

tontine.n.01 tontine, tontine insurance "a form of life insurance whereby on the death or default of a participant his share is distributed to the remaining members"
