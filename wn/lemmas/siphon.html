siphon

siphon.n.02 siphon, syphon "a tubular organ in an aquatic animal (especially in mollusks) through which water can be taken in or expelled"

siphon.n.01 siphon, syphon "a tube running from the liquid in a vessel to a lower level outside the vessel so that atmospheric pressure forces the liquid through the tube"

siphon.v.02 siphon "move a liquid from one container into another by means of a siphon or a siphoning action"

siphon.v.01 siphon, syphon, siphon off "convey, draw off, or empty by or as if by a siphon"
