Basidiomycota

basidiomycota.n.01 Basidiomycota, subdivision Basidiomycota, Basidiomycotina, subdivision Basidiomycotina "comprises fungi bearing the spores on a basidium; includes Gasteromycetes (puffballs) and Tiliomycetes comprising the orders Ustilaginales (smuts) and Uredinales (rusts) and Hymenomycetes (mushrooms, toadstools, agarics and bracket fungi); in some classification systems considered a division of kingdom Fungi"
