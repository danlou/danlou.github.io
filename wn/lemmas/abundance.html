abundance

abundance.n.01 abundance, copiousness, teemingness "the property of a more than adequate quantity or supply"

abundance.n.03 abundance "(chemistry) the ratio of the total mass of an element in the earth's crust to the total mass of the earth's crust; expressed as a percentage or in parts per million"

abundance.n.02 abundance "(physics) the ratio of the number of atoms of a specific isotope of an element to the total number of isotopes present"
