telephone.n.01 telephone, phone, telephone set "electronic equipment that converts sound into electrical signals that can be transmitted over distances and then converts received signals back into sounds"

phone.n.02 phone, speech sound, sound "(phonetics) an individual sound unit of speech without concern as to whether or not it is a phoneme of some language"
