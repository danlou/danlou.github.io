Gipsy

gypsy.n.02 Gypsy, Gipsy, Romany, Rommany, Romani, Roma, Bohemian "a member of a people with dark skin and hair who speak Romany and who traditionally live by seasonal work and fortunetelling; they are believed to have originated in northern India but now are living on all continents (but mostly in Europe, North Africa, and North America)"
