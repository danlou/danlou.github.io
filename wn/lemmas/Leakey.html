Leakey

leakey.n.03 Leakey, Louis Leakey, Louis Seymour Bazett Leakey "English paleontologist whose account of fossil discoveries in Tanzania changed theories of human evolution (1903-1972)"

leakey.n.02 Leakey, Mary Leakey, Mary Douglas Leakey "English paleontologist (the wife of Louis Leakey) who discovered the Zinjanthropus skull that was 1,750,000 years old (1913-1996)"

leakey.n.01 Leakey, Richard Leakey, Richard Erskine Leakey "English paleontologist (son of Louis Leakey and Mary Leakey) who continued the work of his parents; he was appointed director of a wildlife preserve in Kenya but resigned under political pressure (born in 1944)"
