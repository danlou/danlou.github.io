ace_inhibitor.n.01 ACE inhibitor, angiotensin-converting enzyme inhibitor "an antihypertensive drug that blocks the formation of angiotensin II in the kidney, leading to relaxation of the arteries; promotes the excretion of salt and water by inhibiting the activity of the angiotensin converting enzyme; also used to treat congestive heart failure"
